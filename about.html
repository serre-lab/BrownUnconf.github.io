Frequently Asked Questions

Frequent Questions

Who is the conference intended for?
All members of the Brown community are welcome!
How do I submit an abstract?
If you'd like to submit an abstract, please fill out the form
How are you streaming the conference?
The conference will take place on Crowdcast. Instructions and links will be sent out prior to the conference.
Is there a registration fee?
The conference is free for all members of the Brown community.
Can I attend the conference even if I don’t want to present?
Absolutely! While we encourage participants to present at the Unconference, as it is great practice and an informal, multi-disciplinary setting, you most definitely don’t need to submit, and we’ll be glad to have you in the audience (and asking questions we hope!)
